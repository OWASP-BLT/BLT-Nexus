What is BLT Nexus?

BLT Nexus is the ambassador program for OWASP BLT, the Bug Logging Tool. Our ambassadors are security professionals and enthusiasts who bridge the gap between BLT and the wider cybersecurity ecosystem.

By becoming an ambassador, you represent a cybersecurity project in the BLT community — helping users report bugs, spreading awareness, and earning recognition for your contributions to making the web more secure.

This page lists all active projects and the ambassadors who champion them. Want to add your project or become an ambassador? Open an issue or pull request on GitHub.

How It Works

1

Apply as Ambassador

Choose a cybersecurity project you're passionate about and apply through the BLT ambassador portal.

2

Connect the Project

Link the project to BLT so community members can report bugs and track issues through our platform.

3

Build the Bridge

Engage with both communities, triage reports, and help projects improve their security posture.

4

Earn Recognition

Top ambassadors earn BLT badges, points, and recognition on this page and across the OWASP community.
